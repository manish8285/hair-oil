How To Use

Short (for comb applicator bottle):

Shake well before use.

Squeeze the bottle and apply oil directly to the scalp using the comb applicator.

Gently massage with fingertips for 3–5 minutes.

Leave for 1–2 hours or overnight. Use 2–3 times weekly for best results.

For external use only. Store in a cool, dry place. Keep out of reach of children.
